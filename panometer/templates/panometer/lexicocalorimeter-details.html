About the Lexicocalorimeter

The foundational research behind the Lexicocalorimeter has been published in PLoS ONE "The Lexicocalorimeter: Gauging public health through caloric input and output on social media" .
In addition to the interactive dashboards and maps availabe on this site, the online appendices for the paper with the data sets used in the study are available at http://www.uvm.edu/storylab/share/papers/alajajian2015a/index.html, and more details on the Panometer are right over on the About page.
