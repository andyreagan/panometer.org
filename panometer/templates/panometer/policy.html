Hedonometer
Hedonometer.org is an instrument that measures the happiness of large populations in real time. The hedonometer is based on people’s online expressions, capitalizing on data-rich social media, and measures how people present themselves to the outside world.

Privacy Policy

The Hedonometer Team respects the privacy of its site users. This privacy policy explains the types of information collected from site visitors, and how we use that information. The use of the site constitutes acceptance of this policy.

Information Collected Automatically

When users visit the Hedonometer site, the site server logs basic information about each visit. We process this information monthly via an automated software tool to identify any site performance issues, popular sections and content, and other important site characteristics. This information does not identify you personally. This information is used only as a source of anonymous statistical information.

The raw log data is retained for a minimum of 90 days.

Information Collected from E-mail and Web Forms

Users may contact us using information found on the About. We may share the information that you provide to us via e-mail within the Hedonometer Team to respond to your queries, but we do not provide information to anyone outside of the Team unless required by law to do so. We will never share your information with a third party for any commercial purpose.

Information Collected from Third Party Software

When users visit the Hedonometer site, users may link to third party software when they link to another party's website. We do not collect any information that may be collected by that third party; however, information you supply to that third party software may be collected and/or used by that party. For information about that third party's Privacy Policy, please see their respective website.

Information Collected from Third Party Media Sites

We currently use Facebook, Google, Twitter, YouTube and Vimeo as third party media sites. We do not collect any information that may be collected by that third party; however, information you supply to that third party software may be collected by that party. For information about that third party's Privacy Policy, please see their respective website.

Information Collected from Smartphone Applications

We do not collect any information that may be collected by a smartphone application; however, information you supply to that smartphone application may be collected by that party. For information about that third party's Privacy Policy, please see their respective website.
